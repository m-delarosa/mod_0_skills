How to become a Turing student

1. Complete application process
  1.1 Provide basic identity and contact information
    1.1.1 Open browser on your computer
      1.1.1.1 Press command + spacebar at the same time to access spotlight search
        1.1.1.1.1 Place middle finger of left hand on command key
        1.1.1.1.2 Press down middle finger until full stop
        1.1.1.1.3 Hold your middle finger in this position
        1.1.1.1.4 Place  the pointer finger of your left on the spacebar key directly to the right of the command key
        1.1.1.1.5 Press down pointer finger of left hand until spotlight search appears on screen
      1.1.1.2 When spotlight search opens, type "Chrome"
        1.1.1.2.1 Locate the "c" key on keyboard
          1.1.1.2.1.1 Look down
            1.1.1.2.1.1.1 Pivot head in downwards motion until keyboard is in line of sight
            1.1.1.2.1.1.2 Scan keyboard with eyes top-down, left-right until "c" key is located
            1.1.1.2.1.1.3 Proceed to step 1.1.1.2.2
        1.1.1.2.2 Press the "c" key
          1.1.1.2.2.1 Place pointer finger of left hand on "c" keyboard
          1.1.1.2.2.2 Apply downward pressure on "c" key with point finger until full stop
        1.1.1.2.3 Locate the "h" key on keyboard
          1.1.1.2.3.1 Scan keyboard with eyes, top-down, left-right until "h" key is located
        1.1.1.2.4 Press the "h" key
          1.1.1.2.4.1 Lift pointer finger off of the "c" key
          1.1.1.2.4.2 Reposition pointer finger above the "h" key
          1.1.1.2.4.3 Apply downward pressure on "h" key with point finger until full stop
        1.1.1.2.5 Repeat steps 1.1.1.2.3 - 1.1.1.2.4 replacing "c" key for "h" key and replacing "h" with "r" key
      1.1.1.3 Press enter
  1.2 Provide Work History
  1.3 Complete Logic Quiz
2. Sign and accept enrollment agreement
3. Pay $1200 deposit
